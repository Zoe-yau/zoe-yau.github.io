HTTPS connection

   HTTPS is a secure connection for websites that have the purposes of ensuring a user's security and privacy. However, HTTPS connection encryptyion growing use has made it harder and more limited for people such as the FBI, schools and others that used traditional network traffic monitoring and filtering (Gibson). In order to get around that they use devices known as HTTPS Proxy Appliances which are a way for people such as the FBI to look into people's activity on the internet.

MITM

   MITM is a man in the middle attack.It is a term used for when a perpetrator positions themselves in a conversation between a user and an application (Imperva, 2019). They do this either to eavesdrop or to impersonate one of the parties making the situation seem like a normal exchange. It is risky because whatever information is obtained by the perpetrator could be used for ill-purposes like identity theft.

Hash

   A Hash is a complex mathematical algorithm which carefully processes every single bit of what they digest The essential qualities that make a has a good hash is that it has sensitive and strong detection of anything changed anywhere in a security certificate. In the case of one bit inside the certificate being changed half of the fingerprint's hash bits will change as well.

SSL Interception Prevention

   The SSL interception can not be prevented but it can be detected because it is not possible to completely imitate any security certificate. Public and private keys form cryptographically matched pairs. The SSL security certificates provide the site's Public key to the public whereas the private one is never disclosed. The interception does not have the private key and they may be able to fake one but it wont match, so the interception is able to be detected.

False-positives and false-negatives

   A false-positive is when massive companies distribute multiple security certificates to different servers and websites globally the comparison of the certificates may lead people to thinking that their connection was intercepted. However they have actually just received a dnnnifferent certificate. A false negative is when their connection was intercepted but was not alerted.

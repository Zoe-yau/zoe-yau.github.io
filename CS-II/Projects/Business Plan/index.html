Available Tables

Identity: Our idea is an app that allows users to find and book available tables in any restaurant.

Problem: Many people have anxiety when calling to book a reservation at restaurants. This creates the risk of having no available tables at that restaurant.

Solution: The app that we have come up with can book a table without having to make a phone call. The app will show an overview of the restaurant's availability during different time intervals. Users will then be able to select which table they want to book during which time.

Target Market: Adults or children that have conset to use the app from a guardian 18 or over.

Competition: Apps such as OpenTable, TheFork, and Resy have simlar ideas.

Revenue Streams: Available Tables will be sold directly to the users using the app.

Marketing Activities: We will use social media, such as twitter and instagram, as well as email to advertise.

Expenses: The expenses will be associated with obtaining materials to create the app as well as advertising.

Team and Key Roles: Currently there are two members, but as the team grows we will need people able to add in restaurants, program booking tables, graphic designers, and advertisers.

Milestones: As the app grows, Available Tables will advertise in target markets and grow and find more effiecent ways to book tables.
